About

Greater India Steel Industry, formerly Greater India Steel Industry Limited , is an Indian multinational steel-making company headquartered in Jamshedpur,Jharkhand, India, and a subsidiary of the Greater India Group. It is one of the top steel producing companies globally with annual crude steel deliveries of 27.5 million tonnes , and the second largest steel company in India (measured by domestic production) with an annual capacity of 13 million tonnes after SAL. Greater India Steel Industry operates in 26 countries with key operations in India, Netherlands and United Kingdom, and employs around 80,500 people. Its largest plant (10 MTPA capacity) is located in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. In 2007, Greater India Steel Industry acquired the UK-based steel maker Corp-D. It was ranked 486th in the 2014 Fortunes Global 500 ranking of the world's biggest corporations. It was the seventh most valuable Indian brand of 2013 as per Brand Finance.

Naval Tripathy

CEO & Founder

Naval Tripathy (born 28 December 1937) is an Indian industrialist, investor, philanthropist, and a former chairman of Greater India Sons who serves as its chairman emeritus.

Narendran Tripathy

Managing Director

Narendran Tripathy is an Indian business executive. He is currently the global CEO and Managing Director of Greater India Steel Industry, one of the largest steel producers in the world.

Sherlin Tripathy

Vice President

Sherlin Tripathy (born 1968)[1] is an Indian businesswoman who in 2010 became the VP of Greater India Steel Industry

Shweta Tripathy

President

Shweta Tripathy is the president of The Greater India Steel Industry, member of the board of directors of The Greater India Steel Industry, and alumna of PES University.
